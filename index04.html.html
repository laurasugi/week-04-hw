Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe


Just like grandma's!

Ingredients
  • Dry Ingredients:
    • 2 1/2 cups of flour
    • 1 tsp. baking soda
    • 1 tbsp. baking powder
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • 1 cup of sugar
  • Wet ingredients
    • 4 Tbsp. butter, melted
    • 1 cup of milk
    • 2 eggs, whole
  • Other
    • 1 cup 1/2 cup of chocolate chips (For lighter version)

    Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 350 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
    2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper or cooking spray and set aside.
    3. Measure all dry ingredients using the scoop and sweep method into a large bowl and whisk together.
    4. Whisk all wet ingredients together in another bowl until smooth.
    5. Slowly fold wet mixture to dry mixture, stirring until fully incorporated.
    6. Gently mix in chocolate chips.
    7. Once all ingredients are mixed, form into one inch wide balls and place on prepared cookie tray, leaving about 2 inches of space between each ball.
    8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
    9. Transfer to a baking rack and allow to cool before eating
    10. Store leftovers in an airtight container and eat within 5 days!
