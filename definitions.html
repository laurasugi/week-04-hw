Definitions
  • Scoop and sweep method- A method for measuring dense powder such as flour. Simply scoop the ingredient gently with your measuring cup, and slide a butterknife along the top to sweep off any excess and leave you with a level cup.
  • Fold-A method for incorporating light ingredients into heavier ones. Not the same as stirring. Place heavier ingredients at the bottom of the bowl and put the lighter one on top. Scoop twords you, underneath the heavy ingredients, and flip them over the top replacing the heavy with the light. Continue to do this until combined, with 1/4 turns of the bowl in between each fold.
